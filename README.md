## 🗄️ Data

**mk_orders (7,175 rows)**  
Contains order-level data: dates, stores, customers, prices, currencies, exchange rates, and converted UAH amounts.

**mk_customers (354 rows)**  
Customer lifecycle data: registration and last purchase dates.

## 📊 Key Metrics

- 💰 Total Turnover: 8.25M UAH  
- 📦 Total Orders: 3,024  
- 👥 Total Customers: 214  
- 🛒 Average Order Value: 2,731 UAH  
- 🔄 Average Purchase Frequency: 14.13  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Seasonality
November is consistently the peak month due to Black Friday.  
Maximum turnover reached ~272K UAH in 2023.

---

### COVID-19 Impact (2020)
April–May 2020 shows a noticeable decline in turnover.

**Possible reasons:**
- strict lockdown restrictions  
- economic uncertainty  
- reduced household income  

→ Customers shifted to a “saving mode” and reduced non-essential spending.

---

### War Impact (2022)
March 2022 marks the lowest point (~6.6K UAH), followed by a relatively fast recovery.

---

### Winter 2025–2026: Demand Crisis
The most challenging period in the business history.

November 2025:  
**63,774 UAH vs 272,454 UAH in 2023 (−76%)**

**Key drivers:**
- prolonged power outages  
- significant currency fluctuations  
- decreased purchasing power  
- shift in consumer priorities toward energy resilience (heating devices, power stations)

→ Result: a sharp decline in both order volume and total revenue.

---

### Market Structure

- 77% — United States (USD)  
- 13% — Europe (EUR)  
- 8% — United Kingdom (GBP)  

→ The business is highly dependent on the US market.

---

### Customer Loyalty

- High purchase frequency: **14+ orders per customer**  
- Core revenue is generated by a **loyal customer base**

→ Indicates strong retention among active users.

---

### Churn

~83% of customers are inactive (no purchases in the last 6+ months).

→ This represents the **largest growth opportunity** for the business.

---

### Top Stores

Top-performing stores:
- Macy’s (leader)  
- Michael Kors  
- Victoria’s Secret  

→ Brand-driven demand plays a significant role in sales performance.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Customer Reactivation
- Launch targeted reactivation campaigns (email / Telegram)  
- Provide personalized product selections  
- Offer exclusive incentives for inactive customers  

---

### 2. Crisis Adaptation Strategy
- Expand product mix with essential goods (heating, power solutions)  
- Emphasize value and savings in communication  
- Adjust assortment based on economic conditions  

---

### 3. Currency Risk Diversification
- Increase focus on European retailers  
- Highlight favorable exchange rates in marketing  

---

### 4. Loyalty Program Development
- Introduce a loyalty program with rewards  
- Provide early access to promotions  
- Create VIP segmentation for top customers  

---

### 5. Seasonal Strategy Enhancement
- Strengthen pre-Black Friday campaigns  
- Warm up the audience in advance  
- Maximize revenue during peak periods
